Pricing and volume plans

About our API plans

The free BASIC subscription allows to classify up to 500 names per month (origin, ethnicity) and 5000 names per month (gender).

Subscribe to a PROFESSIONAL, ULTRA or MEGA plan to access online support and a reliable service that will benefit your company/organization.

About subscriptions

Subscribing to a monthly plan reduces the processing cost for larger volumes of data. Overages as calculated according to your current subscription period, billed and reset every month. Subscriptions can be canceled or modified at any time. We don't apply pro-rating to simplify billing and avoid having subscriptions changed too often. We use Stripe for secure payment processing, so we never have access to your CC details.

With NamSor PRO, for example, you pay a monthly subscription of 19$ to process up to 10000 names (to infer gender). Beyond that, the following charges will apply : $0.003 per extra genderized name. Prices are quoted in your preferred currency, excluding tax/VAT. Ask us about ENTERPRISE pricing if you plan to process more than one million names per month.

We apply a (1 / 20) ratio on duplicate names, so you can process up to twenty times the same name.

Individual researchers

Our service is designed for use by organizations (Companies and start-ups, Universities, Government bodies, NGOs, ...), not by individuals. Individual researchers and students should register as a University after getting approval from their supervisor.

European organizations

If you are a company registered in Europe, please update your billing information BEFORE adding a payment method or subscribing to a plan so we can select the appropriate currency. French-registered companies and organizations must specify a valid VAT number FRXXXXXXXXXXX (for example, ours is FR84811488444).
